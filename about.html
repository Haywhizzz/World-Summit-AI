World Summit AI 2020

A brand new online format and theme #withoutborders for 2020

What’s WSAI? The world’s leading and largest AI summit gathers the global AI ecosystem of Enterprise, BigTech, Startups, Investors and Science, the brightest brains in AI as speakers every October in Amsterdam to tackle head-on the most burning AI issues and set the global AI agenda. What’s on in 2020? During these uncertain times and with the safety of the WSAI community in our hearts, we’ve decided to bring the world-class AI brains and unique energy World Summit AI and InspiredMinds is renowned for, in a brand new online format and theme “Without Borders”. We’ve redesigned the summit into a more simple and practical way to consume the same world-class content with 10 mind-blowing online events delivered over a 3 month period, from September to November 2020, as part of the Inspired AI event series. The all new and incredible Inspired AI series has been curated to deliver a jaw-dropping line-up of the world’s greatest experts and speakers and will gather the global AI community in not just 1 but 10 events!

In technology, there is incremental change & fundamental innovation. Developer technology from artificial intelligence to big data represents fundamental innovation that people can build on for years. At this event, there will be companies like, LinkedIn, TedX that will cover the full scope of a software architect's job, from IT to leadership and business skills.
